Welcome to FactGathererAPI!

Serving fun knowledge, one fact at a time.

Usage:

Fact/Fakect: Fact for real facts, fakect for fake facts.

Available routes:

  • /?id=id1,id2...: gives facts/fake facts with specified IDs, all of them if no id is specified.
  • /random?count=number: gives specified amounts of (1 if not specified) random fact/fake facts.
  • /all: gives all facts/fake facts in the database.
  • /count: gives the count of fact/fake facts.
